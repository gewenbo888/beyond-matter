# Beyond Matter · 超越物质

A cinematic, bilingual essay-in-motion on the abstraction of reality —
from earth and fire to atoms, fields, information, and pure structure.

> Reality is becoming less about things — and more about structure.

Eight chapters move through humanity's changing models of the world:
the Ancient World, the Mechanistic Universe, the Atomic, the
Relativistic, the Information Universe, and the still-open Structural
Universe. Each layer is rendered with its own visual logic — a Three.js
particle field that dissolves a stone through atoms, fields and a graph;
a D3 force layout of physical and mathematical concepts; an emergent
Game of Life; a recursive mirror; an observer field that reacts to your
cursor.
